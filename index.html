Why choose us?

Best food in the cities

Hamro Resturant combines flavors & inspiration from the Far East & the West to create what we think is the best! Home to the original (recipe), we specialize in pizzas, as well as handmade appetizers, sandwiches, salads, and gluten friendly, vegetarian, & vegan selections. Feel free to indulge in a tiki drink or craft beer with our fantastic unique cuisine. Cheers!

Hamro Resturant offers menus for Lunch and Dinner. The restaurant has a clasic atmosphere with nepali flok ornaments and music. Within a year of its opening,Hamro Resturant has been recognised for its quality of food and excellent service.
